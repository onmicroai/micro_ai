What are AI Micro-Apps?

AI MicroApps are flexible AI-powered apps that feel as familiar as building out a form. MicroApps are transparent, so you are always in control of your prompt and have the ability to adapt it.

No more “black box”, unchangeable AI solutions. Micro-apps allow organizations to superpower their AI adoption in a brand-consistent way while reducing onboarding and training costs.
